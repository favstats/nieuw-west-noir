# Nieuw-West Noir — Room Tasks

Every 20 minutes, each player receives a new task sending them to a specific room. Tasks are designed to create social interactions, movement through the house, and opportunities for murder.

---

## Balcony (max 8) — 8 tasks

1. Go to the balcony and admire the night sky with someone
2. Step outside for fresh air and share a secret with a guest
3. Have a hushed conversation on the balcony about the evening
4. Psychoanalyze another guest on the balcony
5. Discuss the importance of freedom with a fellow guest
6. Make an exasperated comment about another guest
7. Lean on the railing and confide a fear to whoever is nearby
8. Challenge someone to a staring contest under the stars

## Living Room (max 14) — 8 tasks

1. Check the art in the living room and share your opinion
2. Sit in the living room and discuss your philosophy of life
3. Compliment the living room for its exquisite decor
4. Discuss recent world events with other guests
5. Review the music that is playing, or ask a guest about their musical taste
6. Pick up a book from the shelf and read a passage aloud dramatically
7. Debate whether the painting on the wall is genuine or a forgery
8. Toast to the evening and make a short speech about nothing important

## Kitchen (max 8) — 6 tasks

1. Pretend to make a drink in the kitchen
2. Help yourself to refreshments and chat with whoever is there
3. Offer to prepare something for another guest
4. Critique the wine selection with theatrical authority
5. Tell a scandalous story while leaning against the counter
6. Accidentally knock something over and blame the nearest guest

## Hall (max 5) — 7 tasks

1. Linger in the hall and greet passing guests
2. Have a private conversation in the hallway
3. Stand by the entrance and observe who comes and goes
4. Make another guest self-conscious by complimenting their style too much
5. Make up rumours about what other guests are doing in other rooms
6. Pace dramatically as if waiting for someone who never arrives
7. Whisper something conspiratorial to the first person who walks by

## Big Bathroom (max 3) — 5 tasks

1. Freshen up and have a brief chat near the bathroom
2. Powder your nose and exchange gossip
3. Wait near the bathroom and corner someone for a quick word
4. Stare at yourself in the mirror and question your life choices aloud
5. Practise your alibi in front of the mirror

## Laundry Room (max 3) — 7 tasks

1. Sneak into the laundry room for a secret conversation
2. Pretend to look for something in the laundry room
3. Hide in the laundry room and eavesdrop on nearby chatter
4. Reminisce about "the one who got away" to whoever will listen
5. Admire the boiler engineering with genuine fascination
6. Hide away from the party's hubbub and enjoy the quiet
7. Confess something trivial as if it were a terrible secret

## Gustavo's Bedroom (max 3) — 5 tasks

1. Relax and enjoy the stillness with another guest
2. Curiously hang about in Gustavo's room
3. Discuss spirituality with another guest
4. Confess admiration for another guest's professional achievements
5. Reminisce about old dreams and adventures

## Jakob's Bedroom (max 3) — 5 tasks

1. Relax and enjoy the stillness with another guest
2. Curiously hang about in Jakob's room
3. Discuss spirituality with another guest
4. Confess admiration for another guest's professional achievements
5. Reminisce about old dreams and adventures

## Notes

- Murder target: **10 kills** to win (was 6, increased for longer games)
- Tasks are assigned randomly, but never the same room twice in a row
- A new task auto-assigns every 20 minutes, or players can tap "New Task" manually
- Room capacity is shown on the task card as guidance
